About

I am an undergraduate intern at MCML(Multimedia Computing and Machine Learning) Group under the supervision of Prof. Jong-Seok Lee.


My research interests lie in understanding how deep learning models learn and behave. Currently, I am researching the representation space of image classification models, with a focus on feature distribution across various architectures using different regularizations. Through this research, I aim to gain insight into the decision-making process of classification models and explain why their calibration and vulnerability to adversarial attacks vary.
