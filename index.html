Jonghyun Park

I'm a M.S./Ph.D. student in Interdisciplinary Program in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Perception and Reasoning Lab. at Seoul National University advised by Prof. Jonghyun Choi. My research focuses on enhancing safe reasoning in multimodal large language models (MLLMs) through efficient methods, with the goal of creating helpful and reliable multimodal AI systems.

I received B.S. from Yonsei University under the supervision of Prof. Jong-Seok Lee.
